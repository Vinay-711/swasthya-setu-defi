# Research Summary

## Key Findings for SwasthyaSetu

**The Stack**: FastAPI backend, React PWA frontend, PostgreSQL/MongoDB hybrid, heavy use of OpenAI Whisper and XGBoost.
**Table Stakes**: Offline-first, voice interactions in regional languages, portable digital IDs via ABHA.
**Watch Out For**: Connectivity dropouts, over-promising AI diagnostic capabilities, ignoring feature-phone users. Ensure strict DPDP Act consent compliance.

The domain demands extreme accessibility. Low UX friction and robust async processing are more important than real-time synchronization.
