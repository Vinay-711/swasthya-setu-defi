# Domain Research: Pitfalls (Occupational Migrant Health)

## Common Mistakes
1. **Assuming Data Connectivity**: Health centers or brick kilns frequently have zero cellular reception. *Prevention*: Service worker caching and delayed async syncing.
2. **"Tech-Bro" Medical AI**: Replacing doctors instead of augmenting them. *Prevention*: Ensure AI models only "Flag & Recommend Screening" rather than "Diagnosing".
3. **Ignoring Feature Phones**: Building only for smartphones leaves behind the poorest 30% of migrants. *Prevention*: Ensure core alerts function entirely over IVR and SMS without the app.
4. **State-Locked Data**: Integrating with only one state's portal. *Prevention*: Use ABDM/ABHA national rails to ensure portability.
