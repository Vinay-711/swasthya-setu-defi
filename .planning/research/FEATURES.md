# Domain Research: Features (Occupational Migrant Health)

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Portable Identity**: ABHA/QR-based health passport that works across state lines.
- **Voice/Language Interfaces**: Audio input/output in local regional languages.
- **Offline Reliability**: Core functionality must work without an active internet connection, syncing when connectivity is restored.
- **Document Digitization**: Camera-based OCR for old paper records.

## Differentiators
- **Predictive Occupational Risk Engine**: Forecasting diseases like silicosis or byssinosis before they happen based on job profile.
- **Care Continuity & ASHA Routing**: Automatically re-routing community health worker follow-ups when a migrant moves to a new state.
- **Omnichannel Notifications**: WhatsApp + SMS + Voice Calls (IVR) fallback cascade for critical alerts.

## Anti-Features (Do Not Build)
- **Heavy mobile apps**: Avoid 50MB+ downloads.
- **Text-heavy interfaces**: Forms requiring manual typing will fail.
- **Centralized non-consensual tracking**: Strict DPDP compliance required. Data must be worker-owned.
